## 🔑 Setting Up nightwire with Your Phone

nightwire uses Signal on your phone to send and receive coding data securely. To link your phone with nightwire on your PC, follow these steps:

1. **Ensure Signal is installed on your phone**

   Download and install Signal from your phone’s app store if you have not already.

2. **Open nightwire on your PC**

   When you launch nightwire, it will prompt you to connect your device.

3. **Use the QR Scanner in Signal**

   On your phone, open Signal and go to ‘Settings’ > ‘Linked Devices’.

   Tap ‘Link New Device’ and use your phone to scan the QR code shown in the nightwire app on your PC.

4. **Approve connection**

   Confirm the connection on your phone. This links the two devices securely.

After setup, your phone and PC will communicate safely. You can now start coding via Signal with nightwire.
